Published in: Statistics of Financial Markets

Description: 'Simulates and plots a path of a Cox-Ingersoll-Ross (CIR) process.'

Keywords: cir, graphical representation, interest-rate, plot, process, simulation, stochastic, stochastic-process

- beta : Parameter
- gamma : Parameter
- m : Mean

Output: Plot of a simulated CIR process
